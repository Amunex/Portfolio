About me

Motion Designer

My name is Matías Amunátegui. I’m a dual American and Chilean citizen with a bachelor’s degree in Digital Design, also called Multimedia Studies.

I was born and raised in Chile and recently moved the the United States of America. My cultural knowledge and the amount of traveling I've done in my life gives me an edge when it comes to communication with foreigners, and a versatile tool for designing as a whole.

I’ve worked since I was young to become a storyteller skilled in using all forms of media available to me. It’s still my highest aspiration, and something I passionately work on to improve. My hobbies stretch far and wide, from space and science all the way to music and graphic novels.


I've become a passionate in my field of work,  and I consider my self a fast paced worker that's used to working with tight deadlines yet always excited to toss out ideas and concepts, develop them, and feel proud once the task is done.

My best work is unfortunately is under NDA. My current Reel is mostly comprised of my motion graphics hobby that I worked in conjunction  with several different talented artists. Lately I’ve been working motion comics as a side hobby. This is where I really enjoy my work as these projects require all of my skills to generate.

Fell free to brows through my website, click on all the videos and watch them to your content/grab inspiration. If you are hungry for more, I have several other videos uploaded into my YouTube Gallery
